SafeStay

A hackathon project built around one rule: a hotel that fails fire and structural safety checks cannot be booked through this platform — no matter the price or rating.

Why this exists

Built in response to recent hotel fire incidents in Delhi, where missing fire NOCs and blocked emergency exits cost lives. Safety data shown here is demo data for hackathon purposes.

Demo disclaimer

Hotel names, certificates, and scores are simulated for this prototype and do not reflect real properties or real government records.
