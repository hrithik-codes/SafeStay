The method

How a hotel earns the right to be booked

Step 1 — Hard gate

Fire NOC, working extinguishers, marked exits, and a fire escape staircase (4+ floors) are mandatory. Miss any one — booking is disabled, full stop.

Step 2 — Weighted score

Hotels that clear the gate get a 0–100 score from sprinklers, alarms, electrical/structural certs, CCTV coverage, lighting, and guard coverage.

Step 3 — Traveller lens

The same data is re-weighted for who's staying — female safety, child-proofing, group rooms — so the score reflects your actual trip.
